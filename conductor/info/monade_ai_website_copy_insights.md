---

### 1.2 What Your Platform Actually Does (Beyond "simple setup")

You're not selling "no-code voice AI." You're selling:

**"Pre-engineered conversation architecture with built-in sales psychology"**

Specific unique capabilities from your research:

1. **8-Stage Conversation Architecture** (not random chat)
   - Opening (permission gate), Rapport, Discovery, Presentation, Objection Handling, Closing, Follow-up, Referrals
   - Each stage has duration targets, branching logic, and cultural guardrails

2. **Adjacency Pair Engineering** (from Conversation Analysis theory)
   - Every AI question is designed to produce a useful next turn
   - Binary/bounded questions guide toward qualification
   - Example: NOT "Any thoughts?" → "Which matters more: reducing missed calls or improving response time?"

3. **Cultural Compliance Layer** (TRAI + Indian market dynamics)
   - Honorific protocols (Name ji, sir/ma'am, 'aap' never 'tum')
   - Code-switching rules (when to use English vs Hindi)
   - Family decision embrace (not obstacle handling)
   - WhatsApp qualification (not brush-off)

4. **Psychological Persuasion Sequence** (research-backed, ethical only)
   - Loss framing before gains (Prospect Theory)
   - Anchoring with credible ranges (Tversky & Kahneman)
   - Micro-commitment chains (Cialdini)
   - Autonomy preservation (reduces reactance)
   - Pre-emptive inoculation (McGuire theory)

5. **AI-Specific Conversation Mechanics**
   - Latency management (400-1200ms filler systems)
   - Interruption handling protocols
   - Repair sequences for misunderstanding
   - Backchanneling instructions (hmm, achha, right)
   - Reconnection protocols for silence

**Copy strategy:** Frame these as "The difference between a voice AI that sounds like IVR hell and one that actually books appointments."
