Competitors give you a blank canvas and expect you to figure out conversation design. Monade gives you **pre-engineered scripts with built-in sales psychology, Indian cultural compliance, and AI-specific mechanics.**

## Part 6: Copy Rules (For All Future Pages)

### The 7 Rules of Monade Copy

1. **Lead with insight, not features.** Don't say "We have AI voice agents." Say "Most voice AI gets hung up on because it ignores conversation science."

2. **Show the mechanism.** Don't say "high conversion." Say "43:57 talk ratio, loss framing, micro-commitment chains."

3. **Be specific about India.** Don't say "works globally." Say "Hindi-English code-switching, honorific protocols, family decision embrace."

4. **Pair every claim with evidence.** Don't say "research-backed." Say "Schegloff telephone openings, Kahneman loss framing, Gong.io 43:57 ratio."

5. **One idea per section.** Each bento card owns exactly one message. If you need two, you need two cards.

6. **Write for the person who signs the check.** VP of Sales. Operations Head. Founder who's tired of voicemails. Speak their language: revenue, pipeline, cost-per-lead, booking rate.

7. **Kill generic B2B speak.** Replace "seamless integration" with "Direct CRM sync — Google Sheets, Notion, Zoho." Replace "AI-powered" with "8-stage conversation architecture with built-in sales psychology."

### Tone Calibration

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|-------|---------|
| "You have 8 seconds before they hang up." | "Optimize your customer engagement." |
| "43:57 talk ratio. Your prospect talks more." | "Advanced conversational AI capabilities." |
| "Week 1 script ≠ Week 12 script." | "Continuous AI-powered optimization." |
| "₹0 platform fee. Per-second billing." | "Flexible and affordable pricing." |
| "Family consultation is a real step, not an objection." | "Handle customer objections effectively." |
| "Loss frame first: show what they're losing." | "Leverage cutting-edge persuasion technology." |
