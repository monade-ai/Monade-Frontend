# Product Definition

## Vision
Voice Intelligence for the Next Billion Operations. Monade AI aims to humanize operations by providing the world's most precise Hinglish NLP for high-stakes voice workflows.

## Target Audience
Indian businesses and enterprises requiring scalable, auditable, and human-grade voice automation (e.g., banking, logistics, sales, customer support).

## Core Values
- **Precision:** Accurate Hinglish NLP that understands code-switching nuances.
- **Speed:** Latency as a feature (targeting ~142ms).
- **Compliance:** Bank-grade security, PII redaction, and regulatory adherence (RBI/GCP).
- **Scale:** Infrastructure capable of handling thousands of concurrent calls.

## Key Features
- **The Cockpit:** Total operational control with real-time monitoring of every syllable and compliance checkpoint.
- **True Hinglish:** Understanding the nuance of Indian business talk and Tier 3 dialects.
- **Bank-Grade Compliance:** Auto-redaction of PII and automated scoring against regulatory rubrics.
- **Voice Gallery:** High-quality, human-grade voice samples for various industry use cases.
- **Exploded View:** Transparency and auditability into how the AI processes voice and data.
